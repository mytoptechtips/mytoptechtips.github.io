Quizwordz

The aim of the game is to guess the title

You will be given a series of clues to identify a show. Type your guesses into the boxes and press the Guess button

As the game progresses more and more of the picture is revealed. You can click on the exposed tiles to zoom into them.

With each guess your points total goes down.

You can share a link to the game using the menu "Share" link, or you can just copy the URL from your browser.
